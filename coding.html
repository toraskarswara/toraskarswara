My Coding Projects





My coding journey started when I was a 13-year-old girl desperate to get out of the house because of the lockdown. I was quite bored and had nothing much to do. then there I was exploring the internet while I came across the coding platform and since then there was no turning back. I learned C and C++ languages within no time. It was later after my 10th grade when our math and physics professor introduced us to another coding platform ‘GEOGEBRA’ where we started programming to learn the concepts of physics and math. Every Sunday we gathered together to do coding projects. We call them our ‘Sunday Side Projects’. And now finally we have started with HTML and CSS where we have learned many new things from all our teachers. My overall journey was good so far. I am keen on learning more on the upcoming Sundays.
